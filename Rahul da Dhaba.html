Ice Cream

Ingredient:

  1. Milk
  2. Sugar
  3. Choco Powder
  4. Flavour

Recipe:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of heavy cream and 1 can of sweetened condensed milk.

2. Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract and any desired flavorings (chocolate chips, fruit, etc.).

3. Gently fold the ingredients together until well mixed.

4. Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and cover with plastic wrap.

5. Freeze for at least 4-6 hours, or until the ice cream is firm. Enjoy!

Pizza

Ingredient:

  1. Flour
  2. Salt
  3. Cheese
  4. Tomato ketchup

Recipe:

1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).

2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer to a baking sheet.

3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough and top with shredded mozzarella cheese.

4. Add your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, veggies, or olives.

5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Enjoy!
